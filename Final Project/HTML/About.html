About Indowest

About Us

At Indowest, we specialize in bringing together the beauty of Indian traditions and the modern fashion of Western culture into one store.

Our Story

Indowest was founded by Vishal and Marshall, two very culturally different students. However, they share a love for fashion and wanted to change how others might view each other's countries. Since they were from very different countries, they made it a priority to learn the differences and similarities of the clothing from their respective countries. From there, the rest is history, and Indowest was born.

Our Goal

At Indowest, our goal is to open the eyes of Canadian and Indian people and show them how beautiful both styles of clothes are and to ensure that our customers are in the best quality clothing and feel confident while staying close to their roots or even trying something new. We want to make it accessible to everyone.
